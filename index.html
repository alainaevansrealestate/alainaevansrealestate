About Alaina

Your Hamilton Home Finding Partner

I'm Alaina Evans — a Realtor® with Coldwell Banker Community Professionals, and a proud, lifelong Ancaster local. Real estate is more than transactions for me. It's about people, neighbourhoods, and finding the place where your life happens.

Growing up in Ancaster gives me deep-rooted knowledge of Hamilton, Dundas, Ancaster, and the surrounding communities — and a genuine passion for helping people find their place in them. I bring honest guidance and real enthusiasm to every buyer and seller I work with.

My goal is to build lasting relationships and make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible — whether you're a first-time buyer, an experienced investor, or ready to sell. I'm in your corner from first conversation to closing day.

When I'm not in real estate mode, you'll find me on the yoga mat — I'm also an instructor — or on the ski hill with my three kids.
